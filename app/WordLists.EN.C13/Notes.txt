Notes:

There are four spelling schemes provided:
en-GB		Spellings in use in the United Kingdom, and generally in most Commonwealth countries and Ireland.
en-GB-oed	Oxford English Dictionary spellings.  Some organizations have adopted this as a standard.
en-CA		Spellings in use in Canada.
en-US		Spellings in use in the United States.

The *words* files contain lists of words with correct spellings.  Words preceded by '!' are only correct in the exact form provided; these are symbols such as pH, kg, etc. which are not subject to capitalization.  (Note these are symbols, not abbreviations.) 

The *repl* files contain misspelled words and the corresponding substitutions.

The medical word lists include the general vocabularies.
